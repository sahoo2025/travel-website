Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a travel package?

You can book through our website by selecting your preferred package and clicking "Book Now", or call our customer service team for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, net banking, UPI, and EMI options for your convenience.

Can I customize my travel package?

Yes! We offer customizable packages. Contact our travel experts to create a personalized itinerary based on your preferences.

What is included in the package price?

Package inclusions vary by destination. Generally includes accommodation, meals as specified, transfers, and sightseeing tours.

Do you provide travel insurance?

Yes, comprehensive travel insurance is available for all our packages. We highly recommend purchasing it for your safety.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by package. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days before travel receive a full refund minus processing fees.
